I Am Number Four

The I Am Number Four series presents itself as an exciting and refreshing take on the teenagers with superpowers trope.

It focuses on the internal struggles and demons each character has to fight to but does not forget the flow of the main story too.

Nine children are sent to Earth to continue the battle against the hostile alien race, the Mogadorians, seeking to restore their former homeland and protect their new home, Earth. They are the Lorics, superpowered beings with the goal to preserve order and peace.

I Am Number Four

The beginning of it all. The prologue to the series where we get introduced to one of the story's main characters, John Smith or Number Four living a secretive life until he finds a friendship with Sam Goodeand a kindling romance with Sarah Hart.

The Power of Six

The second installation. After the events of the first book, John masters his newfound powers and travels with Sam and Number Six, who found and aided him in the final battle of the first book. Together, they find new allies and enemies fueling their mission.

The Rise of Nine

The third installation of the book, after the thrilling conclusion of the second book, John gets separated with Sam and escapes with his newfound ally, the brash but powerful Number Nine. Six, after parting ways with John/Four to investigate a Loric sighting, teams up with Marina AKA Number Seven, Number Eight and surprisingly Ella AKA Number Ten who was an additional child to aid them in their mission.

The Fall of Five

The fourth installation, it marks the two teams meeting up at the conclusion of the third book and battling their greatest enemy for the first time, Setrakus Ra. This also marks them finding the last of their kind, Number Five.

The Revenge of Seven

After a sudden betrayal by Number Five, the team is broken and hopeless after losing Eight, Seven plans revenge, Nine is overcome by guilt and regret, Six is driven by rage and bloodlust, Ten confused and shattered trying to keep the team intact, while Four has a hard time after falling into a coma at the events of the fourth book, after being attacked by Mogadorians and being saved by a traitor pure-blood Mog, Adam.

The Fate of Ten

After reuniting up once again, the team is forced to separate once again, one team to awaken the ancient Loric energy hiding beneath Earth's veins and one team to halt the advance of Mogadorians tricking and invading the city of New York and the biggest states around the world.

United as One

After suffering a tragic loss with the death of Sarah Hart and being trapped into a coner after the Mogs succesfully invading, Number Four is driven by the quest to finally end their mission, with the help of the human government, his fellow Loric Garde, and the newly turned human Garde teenagers granted abilities by the Loric energy hiding beneath Earth.
